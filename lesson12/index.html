Purpose:

the Chamber of Commerce of Jaboticabal - was founded in 1932 to contribute to the development of the municipality. It represents more than 200 companies of all sizes and segments. Among its responsibilities, it develops the defense of the interests of the business community, the provision of services to members, its training and the promotion and support for the development of business environments favorable to all sectors of the economy of Jaboticabal.

Goals:

  • Defending business interests
  • Education,Empower, Encourage and support the development of business environments
  • Guide, as an advisory body to the Municipal Government, in defining the municipality's holiday calendar and in matters of interest to the business community.
  • Work with government agencies, form partnerships and formulate actions to strengthen the class and the business of its members.
