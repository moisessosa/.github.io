Target Audience:

This weather site may be visited by the general public, but it will be especially focused on people who need to know the weather forecast, to plan their professional activities, they will be people who work in planning events, in carrying out outdoor activities such as camping, and cross country sports.

Scenarios and Personas

Name:Sami Muro
Age: 30
Gener: Woman
Occupation:Bachelor degree in Advertising and Marketing
Purpose of Visit: Know the weather forecast, to take preventive measures for the events that she organizes outdoors, for example if the forecast is for rain she can place more awnings, or if it is very hot make more water available.
Scenario:She will consult the web from her Tablet or phone, which are her two main work tools.

Name:Tobias Lupton
Age: 35
Gener: Man
Occupation:Personal trainer
Purpose of Visit: Plan individual and group excursions and workouts outdoors
